Front-End Developer, with a love for creating, from Milwaukee WI.

Hello, my name is Kyle Orlinski and I am a Front End Developer. I currently specialize in AngularJS, and my work is focused on using ES6. When it comes to design and programming, I focus primary on simplicity and usability.

In the last 12 months I have completely focused on front end development, and made it my goal to become an expert in such a broad career path. Before my focus I found my passion from general programming in small projects and creating JavaScript games. Currently I am working at Bon-Ton as a Front End Developer in downtown Milwaukee.

Now I want to shift my focus on moving up to Angular 2 with TypeScript to help me focus on creating modular web applications.
