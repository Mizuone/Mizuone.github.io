Hi, I am Kyle Orlinski.

Software & Indie Game Developer

About

Career

It began in late 2016 as a contractor at Boston Store, where I worked as a JavaScript developer to create a mega menu navigation system for their E-commerce platform. In 2017, I was hired full-time and continued developing site features, including a store locator, coupon automation checkout, and a promotional video player.

After nearly two years, I joined Concurrency, Inc. as an associate software developer on their modern apps team. During this time, I collaborated with talented developers and sharpened my skills in building enterprise software, specializing in Angular, Azure, and TypeScript. In late 2019, I was promoted to software developer, expanding my expertise to include .NET, React, and various supporting technologies. Throughout my career at Concurrency, I contributed to a variety of web applications, including SaaS, B2B, B2C, and Fintech solutions.

At the end of 2022, I took a break from my software career to explore new directions and determine the best path for my next step. Currently, I am actively seeking opportunities with companies that align with my career goals. I spend my days exploring new software technologies and building side projects. Additionally, I dedicate time to prototyping games in Unity, focusing on developing a co-op action RPG.

Personal

My personal life is filled with several hobbies, including weightlifting and practicing MMA. When I'm not training, I focus on building my RPG game. Additionally, my curiosity leads me to explore various other activities.
